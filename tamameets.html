Various Apps for Tamagotchi Meets & On

Various Apps to get extra content into your Tamagotchi Meets & On.

MixingMeets

What is MixingMeets?

Replacement of the Official Tamagotchi Meets/On App.

MixingMeets is a fan-made project to provide support and access after the shut-down of the official Tamagotchi ON & Meets app by Bandai on 31 August 2022.

How to download Mixing Meets?

You can read more from the MixingMeets official website FAQ. 

MyMeets Windows App

What is MyMeets app?

The MyMeets app is a fan-made app that allows you to customize a partner for your Tamagotchi Meets to marry, download custom wallpapers, and gain access to items and Gotchi points immediately. It is only for Windows OS. Requirement to run MyMeets app: Bluetooth Low Energy Support OR a Bluetooth 4.0 USB Dongle that supports BLE and Windows 10 FULLY UPDATED

You can download MyMeets App from TAMATOWN WEBSITE.

MYMEETS APP WALKTHROUGH & OTHER FAQ'S - video by samthegreat0827

You can also add your own custom content into the MyMeets app. Here's where you can download all of the M!X items/accessories into your MyMeets app, and then transfer them to your Tamagotchi Meets/On.

Magic Hut & Moon Terrace

THE MAGIC HUT: There is now a special custom made "hack" in the form of a downloadable location called the Magic Hut that you can add into your Tamagotchi Meets/On via the MyMeets App. Upon visiting this location, you can do several things such as:

  1. Instantly make your Tamagotchi an adult
  2. Instantly fill up your TAmagotchi's happiness and hunger
  3. Give you 99,999 Gotchi Points
  4. Enable twins
  5. Unlock ALL locations (helps if there are some locations that usually can only be unlocked with 2 or more Tamagotchis)
  6. Increase all friendship levels to maximum so that every character in the game will propose to you immediately.

MOON TERRACE: It allows you to create a character of your choice to marry. You can control the parts, colors, DNA, twins, gender, and even skip the baby stage! 

You can download both Magic Hut and Moon Terrace here.

Meets Link iOS App

Meets Link is an app for iOS only available in the China App Store (iOS) that is similar to MyMeets without marrying function. It can directly send the items from the mobile app to Tamagotchi Meets. You will need to change your App Store country to China before you can download the app. Link to the app: https://apps.apple.com/hk/app/meets-link/id1590563221
