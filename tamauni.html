How to Obtain Tama Search Characters
Tama Search allows your Tamagotchi to go on a walk to meet NPCs. From the child stage onward, the option can be accessed by pressing the C button in the living room. The Tamagotchi will go out into town and keep walking until the Uni detects a Wi-Fi signal. All signals detected in the area will be turned into an alphanumeric ID and the user can choose one in order to encounter a character. Here is a guide on how to get specific characters via Tama Search.
